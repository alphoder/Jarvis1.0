Quantum Particle Core

Welcome to Aether 3D. You are looking at a simulation of 15,000 particles held in equilibrium by virtual gravity vectors. You can manipulate this space using physical gestures, voice, and keyboard commands.

🤚

Hand Gestures

Grant camera access to enable AI hand-tracking. Try these physical inputs:

  • Pinch (Thumb + Index): Morph and cycle the particle shape.
  • Open/Close Hand: Expand and contract the gravity boundaries.
  • Two Hands Pinch: Scale the Aether core from 0.3x to 3.0x.
🎤

Voice Commands

The system constantly listens for vocal instructions. Speak clearly to trigger actions:

  • 🗣️ "scatter particles" — detonates the core.
  • 🗣️ "reassemble particles" — pulls them back together.
  • 🗣️ "spin faster" / "spin slower" — controls rotation.
💻

Quantum Console & Cheats

Press C or click the top-center connection button to open the Neural Override interface. You can trigger matrix rain, black holes, time freezes, or type codes directly on your keyboard:

  • ⌨️ matrix — activates the falling green binary rain.
  • ⌨️ nuke — detonates a massive light flash.
  • ⌨️ iddqd — initiates full-bloom rainbow spectrum mode.
🎵

Audio Reactivity

Press M to enable microphone feed. Particles will size-modulate and pulse with Bass, Mids, and High frequency thresholds in real time. Try playing music nearby!
